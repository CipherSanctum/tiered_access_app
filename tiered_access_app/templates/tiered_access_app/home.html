Tiered access app example:

The following is an example showing I can create tiered levels of access. If you click any of the following buttons without going through the right process, you won't be able to view what's in it. When you get access, all you get is text saying so. Otherwise it says you don't have access to it yet. This example can be combined with any of the other sample apps I have.

After you see you're denied access to all levels without doing it properly, enable your access by starting on STEP 1. Then I suggest you try them from level 1 and up. Why? Because if you start on level 1, you will only have access to level 1. Level 2 will gave access to 1 and 2. Three gives access to them all. Reset your level to retry it if you want.
